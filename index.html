Dr. Cao is currently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Real Estate and Construction, Faculty of Architecture, The University of Hong Kong (HKU). He graduated with a Bachelor's, Master's, and Doctorate from Tongji University, Stanford University, and ETH Zurich, respectively. Since 2017, he has conducted long-term research in industrialized construction, and contributed to two Swiss National Science Foundation (SNSF) projects. He also leads a Swiss Innovation Fund (Innosuisse) project with Implenia, Switzerland’s largest real estate developer, to develop an “Intelligent Configurator” that shifts the focus from project delivery to product delivery.
