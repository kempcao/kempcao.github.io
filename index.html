Dr. Cao is currently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Real Estate and Construction, Faculty of Architecture, The University of Hong Kong (HKU). He graduated with a Bachelor's, Master's, and Doctorate from Tongji University, Stanford University, and ETH Zurich, respectively. Following his doctoral studies, he held a postdoctoral position at Delft University of Technology (TU Delft). Since 2017, he has conducted long-term research in industrialized construction, contributing to two Swiss National Science Foundation (SNSF) projects. He also leads a Swiss Innovation Fund (Innosuisse) project with Implenia, Switzerlandâ€™s largest real estate developer. Additionally, Dr. Cao has collaborated with global industry partners, including BoKlok (Sweden), Mestergruppen (Norway), ERNE (Switzerland), and Autodesk (U.S.).

He is recruiting PhD/MPhil students, Postdoctoral Fellows/Research Assistants, and Visiting Students/Scholars with interests and expertise in the following areas:

  • Industrialized Construction (also known as Modular Construction or Modern Methods of Construction)
  • Generative Design and Scheduling
  • Digital Twin of Off-site Construction
  • Multi-objective Optimization in the Design, Construction, and Operation Phases
  • Sustainability Analysis in Construction
  • Robotics Adoption in Construction
